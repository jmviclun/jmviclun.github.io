    Research

    Molecular simulation on liquid phase systems and nanoporous materials (ionic liquids, deep eutetic solvents, zeolites, MOFs, COFs,...). Topics included: Gas capture and separation; adsorption, structure, diffusion and characterization. Electrolytes for Dye-Sensitized Solar Cells and Metal-Ion Batteries; density, structure and transport properties. Water solutions; structure, hydrogen bonds analysis, clustering effects
